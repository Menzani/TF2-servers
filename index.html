Why use Mumble?

Did you know that you will hear people before you can see them?
The voice buffer in mumble is 10ms by default. The TF2 client
buffers game state like player movements for 100ms by default,
and even if you change it to the minimum, which is 15.1ms,
that is still a 5ms higher delay!
